About us
Hi there! We are a group of 4 Media and Entertainment students from Breda University, named Karlijn van Baal, Liona Debast, Annemijn van Dongen and Yza Zweers. During our content classes, we came up with the genius idea to create a clothing website made for everyone! Check out ‘our mission’ to learn more about X movement!



Our vision
“in a world where people are being put into boxes regarding to their gender,we believe that everyone should be able to express themselves as diverse as they like, because only then they are free to be the person they are meant to be."



Our mission
X Movement offers a high quality, unique and broad gender-neutral clothing line, fit for the whole family! X movement is a gender-neutral web shop based in The Netherlands, made for everyone. It does not matter what gender, age, religion, rage or size you are; we don’t want people being put into boxes. Our website is clear with a simple design, with not too much fuss and mishmash, because we want people to be who they are, and they don't have to do their best to stand out. Also, sustainability is something very important in the audience needs, and for this reason, we made our clothing environmentally friendly.



Our values

  • Diversity. Everyone is different and special in their own way.
  • Freedom. Everyone should have the freedom to be who they want to be.
  • Fairness. Everyone has the same rights to do whatever they want to do.
  • Self-Respect. How can you love if you don't love yourself?
